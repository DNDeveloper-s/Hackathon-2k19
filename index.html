About Hackathon

Curious?

HACK-A-THON is Techno-knowledge ride organized by IT Department of IMS Ghaziabad University Courses Campus. This inter-institutional Technical Fest will be held on 5th April 2019, which aims to empower the young minds by giving them a rostrum to showcase their genius and innovation. HACK-A-THON bring in a new theme, thus will provide the keen participants a new experience of real time implementations. Students in Technology can actively participate in these events - form diverse teams, make their pitches, adds their expertise and work towards creating a solution. So Come on! HACK YOUR BRAIN!! At the biggest hacking carnival of IMS Ghaziabad, University Courses Campus.

Rules & Regulations

Code Cracker

A coding competition where in coding skills of participants will be judged.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Individual Participation.
  2. Participants should bring their ID Cards.
  3. Every particpant will get same time for a given problem.
  4. Any participant caught cheating will be directly disqualified.
  5. Programs given can be solved in any language (C, C++, Python or JAVA) preferable to the participant.
  6. Every participant should only use the rough papers given to them to do any calculations.
  7. Judges decision would be final decision.

Breach the Firewall

An event to Hack the specified security services (FIREWALL Software System) in IMS Ghaziabad UC Campus. So FIREWALL BREACHERS pull your socks for this wondrous event. Come on BREAK the RULE!!

Rules & Regulations

  1. Individual participation.
  2. Participants should bring their ID cards
  3. Participants should bring their own laptop equipped with suitable softwares for breaching the firewall.
  4. Internet connectivity will be provided.
  5. Scenarios/ Rules will be disclosed on the spot.

Web Development

WEB-A- THON presents an online web development event allowing the students to brainstorm and put their ideas to action. The event covers a series of exciting scenarios that unfold as the competing teams' ace their levels. Students can register in teams of two. So buckle up coders and designers for this thrilling roller coaster ride. It is time to bring your code to life, make your mark and win exciting cash prizes.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Team of 2 members.
  2. Theme will be provided on the spot.
  3. Participants should have command over the language HTML, CSS, PHP and MYSQL.
  4. Only Xampp Server, notepad++ and MS web Expression will be provided at our end.
  5. Coding needed to be start from scratch no web template will be provided.
  6. The participants are also requested to bring their college id cards.

    7. At the end, website will be judged on the basis of:-

    1. Content
    2. Structure & Navigation
    3. Visual Design
    4. Functionality
    5. Interactivity
    6. Innovation
    7. Overall Experience
